Our 40-seat dining room is a masterwork of contemporary Italian design — custom Venetian glass chandeliers, handwoven linen tablecloths, and Murano crystal glassware. The space breathes quiet luxury, with just enough candlelight to make every conversation feel intimate.

The Wine Cellar

Our 2,000-label cellar spans five centuries of Italian winemaking and the finest estates from France, Spain, and the New World. Head Sommelier Marco Ferretti and his team curate every pairing with precision — matching not just the dish, but the emotion of the moment.

2,000+ labels across 18 wine regions
Vertical collections from 1970–present
Award-winning pairing menus
Private cellar tours on request
